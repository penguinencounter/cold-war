The Cold War
U.S. Army photo Photo Credit: USAMHI, Public domain, via Wikimedia Commons
The Cold War was a period of time in which the United States and the Soviet Union fought proxy wars and engaged in a nuclear arms race, but avoided direct attacks due to the threat of Mutually Assured Destruction.
